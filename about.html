At Zubyte, we’re connecting every business to the world’s most skilled digital freelancers, in the simplest way possible.

Zubyte operates all over the world with freelancers and businesses spanning an estimated 160 countries. The company was founded in 2020, is headquartered in Tel Aviv, and has satellite offices in New York, London, Kyiv, Berlin, and Orlando.
